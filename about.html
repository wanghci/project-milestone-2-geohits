GeoHits

Welcome to GEO Hits, a website that allows users to enter a country and find the top trending song. If the user enjoys the song they can add it to their personal site based playlist. Geo Hits was created by Daniel Stewart and Tiago Silva, two rising senior Computer Science majors studying at James Madison University.
